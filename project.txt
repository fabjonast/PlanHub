This project involves developing and designing a digital planner application that helps
users plan, organize, and keep track of their tasks, schedules and goals. This application 
aims to help with time management and productivity by providing an easy-to-use platform
for planning daily, weekly, monthly, or long-term. 
This application is intended for people who have a hard time keeping track of their tasks,
want to create a simple schedule, or plan ahead of time.
In this application users will be able to create, edit, and track tasks, set deadlines,
and view their schedules. Additional features include reminders, notes and progress tracking.
